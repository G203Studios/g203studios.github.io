How to Delete Your Data

If you wish to delete your data, please follow these steps below:

Please note: Your data will be deleted within 7 business days upon us receiving your request.

  • Step 1: Log in to the email account associated to the data within our app.
  • Step 2: Create a new email with the recipient as contact@g203studios.com.
  • Step 3: Set email title to "Data Deletion Request".
  • Step 4: Send email.
